Kaggle: Novozymes Enzyme Stability Prediction - Identify The Thermostable Mutations In Enzymes

This is an ongoing competition by Novozymes held on Kaggle from 21 Sep to 03 Jan that I am currently participating in. The aim of the competition is to come up with a model that predicts the melting point of proteins after they have undergone a single amino acid mutation or deletion. Being able to predict the thermostability of proteins will allow for cheaper and more efficent research that enables advancement in enzyme engineering which will contribute to sustainability and carbon neytrality. <\p>
